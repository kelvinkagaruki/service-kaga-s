# Kaga's Market

A professional, responsive marketplace and delivery platform built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Kaga's Market enables customers to browse and purchase products and services from local businesses with fast delivery.

## Features

- **Product Categories** – Browse products organized by 7+ categories (Groceries, Beauty, Electronics, Home, Fashion, Sports, Books, Services)
- **Product Catalog** – 9+ featured products and services with ratings and vendor information
- **Smart Search** – Real-time search across product titles and vendors
- **Category Filtering** – Quick filter by product category with visual feedback
- **Shopping Cart** – Add, remove, and adjust quantities with live total calculation
- **Delivery Checkout** – Professional order form with customer details, delivery options (Standard/Express), and delivery notes
- **Responsive Design** – Mobile-friendly layout that works on all device sizes
- **Professional UI** – Modern color scheme with gradients, hover effects, and smooth transitions
- **Customer Guidance** – Clear instructions and helpful text throughout the interface

## How to Use

### For Customers
1. Browse products in the **Products** tab or use the **search bar** to find items
2. **Filter by category** to narrow down your selection
3. Click **"Add to Cart"** to purchase items
4. Go to the **Cart** tab to review your order
5. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"** to enter delivery details
6. Choose delivery option and complete your order

### For Developers
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Or run with a local web server: `python -m http.server` (Python) or `npx serve` (Node.js)
3. Or use the included PowerShell server: `.\serve.ps1 -Port 8080`
4. Modify product data in `app.js` to add/remove items
5. Customize colors in `styles.css` CSS variables section
6. Add business posting features in future versions
